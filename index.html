About Us

What Are We ?

We Petals Packaging started our Stretch Wrapping Film Manufacturing and Unit in 2016 at Dadra in Silvassa near Mumbai (Dadra Nagar & haveli 396230) (INDIA) with a capacity of approx. 60-80 MT per Month in 10 to 100 Microns of thickness with different sizes according to the need of Customers.

Stretch Wrapping Film is also Known as Cling Film which is basically used for Food Products and in Hotel Industry, Stretch Film is basically a packing product which is used in 90% of Industries for scratch proofing, UV Protection, Box Packing, Air Tight & Pallet Packing, It is also Used in Pharma Companies in Bulk and at International Airports for Packing Or Wrapping the luggage .
